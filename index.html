Registration and Profile Linking:

All members must first register a profile on our website before being able to participate in matches. By creating an account you are linking your discord, wallet address, game account and region into one unique profile which is used to interact with our bot. Once you have completed the registration process, head over to validation channel in our Discord server and click the “claim role” button to receive your registered player role. the role will give you access to our matchmaking bot of various game modes and queues such as 1v1 and 5v5, available for both EU and NA servers.
(Note: You can only compete in games using the same accounts/wallet used to register. using an in-game account and/or wallet address different than the one used on our registration will result in loss of funds even if you win the match, so be careful).

Matchmaking Bot and Queues:

Our matchmaking bot allows you to jump right into action by simply picking the game/role/server you wish to play with, you are then automatically matched with other players of the same region and adjacent roles. Once enough unique players with adjacent roles and matching servers have been found, our bot creates a new private channel and places all users in it, forming Team #1 and Team #2 assigning each player to a team and also assigning a lobby creator. The channel will be read-only until all 10 players have paid using the embed our bot provides within the channel, once our bot confirms that all players have paid the channel will unlock and the lobby creator will be responsible for sharing the custom game invite.

Payment And Escrow:

Once a new match is started and the bot opens a new private channel for the participants, the channel will contain a "pay" button which players use to checkout using their Solana wallets, you can also check which users have paid by clicking the "check paid" button, once all users have paid the channel will unlock and you'll be ready to head into the game. Competing players wager the same amount which is held by our Escrow Bot until the match results are finalized. Once the match result is processed, the Bot will airdrop the winning team their funds.

Replay Upload and Funds Distribution:

Once a match is finished, the winning team is required to upload the replay of the LoL match to our bot, and it takes that recorded replay and extracts it in "json" file format which contains all relevant information about the played match, such as: The participating players, their in-game characters, their scores, and which team won, distributing the funds amongst winners. The bot will only accept the replay file if the match contains all players that are in the channel, and the teams must be split exactly the same way the bot has placed you.
Example: if our bot placed a player in team #2 in the channel embed, he must also play on team #2 in-game or it may result in loss of funds.

Funds Distribution (Airdrop):

Once the winning team uploads the replay file using the !upload command, within few minutes the bot will extract all the relevant information from the match, compile it into json format and send it as in embed in match channel, then it will airdrop the winning team their funds.
